About eHuza

Connecting families

eHuza project originates from the burning need to contribute on Inmates reintegration process, connecting with families and setting a higher standard to the human rights respect in correctional facilities but also to ease processes of physical visits and management of inmates money transfers where it will resolve a number of challenges including

1

Limited access to Correctional facilities by defenders, families and friends due to both COVID -19 pandemic or other reasons including limited time, cost, physical challenges and logistics

2

Difficulties in transferring money to inmates and managing related transactions

3

Difficulties in managing inmates’ telephone calls.

4

Difficulties to generate Statistics ‘REPORTS’ of visitors, visitee, Frequency of visits, Senders, and Inmates Accounts Balances.

SOLUTIONS

We are committed to contribute on improving the lives of inmates and increasing the services offered by correctional facilities, eHuza project intends to leverage the Africa emerging ICT services and nfrastructure to establish an integrated ICT platform that facilitates:

VALUE & IMPACTS

Provide Inmate necessities and accessibilty to the family

Help Inmates Reintegration into Society

Improve inmates and families mental Health

Promote inmates Right
