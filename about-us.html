About eHuza

Ehuza is a unique platform designed to bring essential services to your fingertips. We understand the importance of staying connected with your loved ones, whether they’re pursuing education in schools or serving time in prison. That’s why we’ve combined two distinct services into one seamless experience.

Our platform offers a range of services for schools, including the ability to send money and book visits to boarding schools. For prisons, we provide options for physical visits, video visits, sending money, and joining video meetings. Each service is designed with user convenience in mind, ensuring you can support and connect with your loved ones with ease. At Ehuza, we believe in the power of connection and support. We’re committed to making it easier for you to maintain these vital links, no matter the distance. Join us on this journey as we continue to innovate and expand our services.
