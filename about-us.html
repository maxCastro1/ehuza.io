About eHuza

eHuza project originates from the burning need to contribute on Inmates reintegration process, connecting with families and setting a higher standard to the human rights respect in correctional facilities but also to ease processes of physical visits and management of inmates money transfers where it will resolve a number of challenges including

Challenges eHuza Solves

1

Limited access to Correctional facilities by defenders, families and friends due to both COVID -19 pandemic or other reasons including limited time, cost, physical challenges and logistics

2

Difficulties in transferring money to inmates and managing related transactions

3

Difficulties in managing inmates’ telephone calls.

4

Difficulties to generate Statistics ‘REPORTS’ of visitors, visitee, Frequency of visits, Senders, and Inmates Accounts Balances.

Our Value & Impacts At eHuza

Provide Inmate necessities and accessibilty to the family

Ensure inmates have access to basic necessities like hygiene products, nutritious meals, and comfortable living conditions. Facilitate strong family ties through regular visitation programs, communication channels, and emotional support services.

Help Inmates Reintegration into Society

Equip inmates with job training, educational opportunities, and mental health resources to build confidence and skills for a smooth transition back into the community. Offer support groups and mentorship programs to foster positive relationships and networks.

Improve inmates and families mental Health

Provide access to therapy, counseling, and support groups for inmates and their families to address past trauma, manage stress, and develop coping mechanisms. Foster emotional intelligence and build a foundation for long-term mental health.

Promote inmates Right

: Advocate for fair and equal treatment of all inmates, ensuring access to legal representation, humane living conditions, and protection from abuse. Promote awareness of inmate rights and educate the public on criminal justice reform.
