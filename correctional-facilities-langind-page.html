Our Services

Built with empathy and driven by innovation, Ehuza simplifies prison communication and strengthens family bonds. Our intuitive platform empowers families and inmates to connect through secure video visits, manage finances seamlessly, and access essential resources – all from the comfort of their homes or within the correctional facility.
