FAQs

This section answers some of the frequently asked questions that users might have about our platform, such as how it works, what are the fees, how to contact support, and more. Find the information you need and get your doubts cleared.

What is Ehuza and how does it work?

Ehuza is a unique platform that combines essential services for schools and correctional facilitates. Whether you want to send money, book a visit, or join a video meeting, Ehuza makes it easy for you to support and stay connected with your loved ones. All you need to do is sign up, choose the service you want, and follow the instructions.

How do I send money through Ehuza?

Sending money through Ehuza is quick and secure. You can send money to your loved ones in school or in corectional facilities using your credit card, debit card, or bank account. Just select the “Send Money” option, enter the amount you want to send, and choose the recipient. You will receive a confirmation email and a receipt once the transaction is completed.

How do I join a video meeting through Ehuza?

Joining a video meeting through Ehuza is practical and inclusive. You can join a video meeting related to your loved ones’ incarceration, such as a parole hearing or a counseling session, using our online platform. Just select the “Join a Video Meeting” option, enter the meeting ID and password, and click on the “Join” button. You will need a device with a camera, microphone, and internet connection to participate.
