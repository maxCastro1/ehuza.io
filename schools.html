Our Services

Built with empathy and driven by innovation, Ehuza simplifies communication with your loved ones in schools. Our intuitive platform empowers parents to connect through secure video visits, manage finances seamlessly, and access essential resources – all from the comfort of their homes.

Physical Visit

This service allows you to schedule physical visits to your loved ones in school, ensuring that you can maintain family, parental connections and provide emotional support.

Video Visit

This service allows you to schedule video visits to your loved ones in school, ensuring that you can maintain family, parental connections and provide emotional support.

Send Money

This service allows you to securely transfer funds to your loved ones in school, ensuring they have access to the resources they need.
